A Voice That Grew With Me

I began studying the flute at the age of seven, drawn to the way a single breath could turn into a clear, singing line. What started as curiosity quickly became the discipline that has shaped my days.

Over the past decade I have performed in school ensembles, regional youth orchestras, and solo recitals. Each performance taught me to listen more carefully — to my own tone, and to the musicians around me.

The flute has given me patience, precision, and a quiet confidence. I hope to carry that voice forward and keep growing as a musician at your school.
